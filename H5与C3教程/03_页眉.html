Architectural Wonders of Barcelona

Barcelona is home to many architectural wonders in addition to Gaudí's work. Some of them include:

  • Arc de Triomf
  • The cathedral (La Seu)
  • Gran Teatre del Liceu
  • Pavilion Mies van der Rohe
  • Santa Maria del Mar
